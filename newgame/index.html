How to Play:

  • Select letters from the grid to form words
  • Words must be at least 3 letters long
  • Letters can be selected in any order (they don't need to be adjacent)
  • Once a letter is used in a found word, it cannot be used again
  • Find as many words as possible in 3 minutes

Scoring System:

  • 3-letter words: 10 points
  • 4-letter words: 20 points
  • 5-letter words: 40 points
  • 6-letter words: 80 points
  • 7-letter words: 160 points
  • 8+ letter words: 200+ points
  • Time bonus: Finding words earlier earns up to 100% bonus points
